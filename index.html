The Rabbit Report



Welcome to the Rabbit Report -- a website all about lagomorphs.

Pet rabbits are lovable and intelligent animals that make great companions for animal lovers of all ages. Bunnies are known for their gentle and affectionate personalities, and they enjoy socializing with their owners. They are easy to care for and are a good choice for people who may not have the space, time, or ability to care for larger pets. When it comes to caring for a pet rabbit, it's important to give them all they need -- including time to play, a healthy diet, a proper hygiene regimen, and a cozy living space. Below, you will find all the information you need to care for your furry friend!
